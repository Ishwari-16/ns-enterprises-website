What is a Rent Agreement?

A Rent Agreement, also known as a Leave & License Agreement, is a legal document between a property owner and a tenant. It clearly defines the rental terms, monthly rent, security deposit, agreement duration, maintenance charges, notice period, and responsibilities of both parties.

In Maharashtra, registration of Leave & License Agreements is an important legal process that helps prevent disputes and provides legal protection to both landlords and tenants.

A registered agreement can be used as proof of residence, for police verification, banking purposes, official documentation, and future legal requirements.
